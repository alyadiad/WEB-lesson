Soul Orange Allows the user to steal, control, manipulate, and alter living and dead souls; as well as animate the motionless. The Soul Gem also acts as a gateway to an idyllic pocket universe. Time Green The Time Stone was one of the six Infinity Stones, the remnants of singularities that predated the universe. It had the ability to manipulate time, even in places "beyond" time, such as the Dark Dimension.

Mind Yellow Loki arrives on Earth armed with the Scepter The Mind Stone was originally portrayed as a power source for the Scepter.
